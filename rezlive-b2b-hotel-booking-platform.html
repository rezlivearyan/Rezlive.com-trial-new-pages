RezLive helps approved travel businesses search, compare and book hotels for leisure, corporate, group and customised travel requirements. The platform combines directly contracted properties with connected global hotel supply so agents can find suitable accommodation across different destinations, budgets and property categories.

What is RezLive's B2B Hotel Booking Portal?

RezLive is a business-to-business hotel reservation platform designed for tourism professionals rather than direct consumers. Travel partners can access global hotel content, review availability and booking conditions, compare trade-focused rates and confirm accommodation for their customers.

Why Travel Businesses Choose RezLive for Hotel Bookings

  • Real-time availability and booking confirmations for eligible hotel inventory.
  • 500,000+ hotel and apartment rooms across worldwide destinations.
  • Directly contracted and connected hotel supply across independent hotels, chains, resorts, boutique properties, hostels and apartments.
  • Booking tools for travel agents, including Autovoucher, booking notes and transparent rate information.
  • RezVault digital wallet support for eligible payments and refunds.
  • API connectivity for eligible OTAs, DMCs and travel technology businesses.
  • Global service support for booking and post-booking requirements.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a B2B hotel booking portal?

A B2B hotel booking portal is a reservation platform created for travel businesses. It allows approved travel professionals to search hotel inventory, check trade rates and book accommodation for their customers.

Who can use RezLive's hotel booking platform?

RezLive is designed for travel agents, OTAs, DMCs, tour operators, wholesalers and corporate travel businesses, subject to registration and approval.

Does RezLive provide real-time hotel availability?

RezLive provides real-time availability and booking confirmation information, subject to the selected hotel's rate and booking conditions.

Can RezLive hotel inventory be connected through an API?

Eligible travel businesses can explore RezLive and RezTez XML or JSON API connectivity for their own website, application or reservation platform.
