What Is RezLive's B2B Rail Booking Platform?

RezLive Rail is a B2B European train booking service for approved travel professionals. It helps agents arrange supported point-to-point train tickets and rail passes for their customers through European rail content supplied by Rail Europe.

Eurail and Interrail Global Pass products cover 33 European countries. Point-to-point train ticket coverage is broader and route-specific, so availability depends on the railway operator, origin, destination, timetable, travel date and selected fare.

Eurail and Interrail: What Is the Difference?

EU

Eurail Pass

Eurail is designed for travellers who are not citizens or legal residents of European countries. Global and eligible one-country options are available.

IN

Interrail Pass

Interrail is designed for citizens or legal residents of European countries. It also provides Global and eligible one-country pass options.

A valid rail pass does not automatically guarantee a seat. Some high-speed, cross-border and night trains require a separate paid seat reservation.

Information to Check Before Confirming a Rail Booking

  • Traveller nationality and country of residence
  • Passenger names exactly as shown on identification
  • Correct departure and arrival stations
  • Travel date, departure time and connection time
  • Passenger age category and eligibility
  • First or second class selection
  • Ticket or pass validity period
  • Seat reservation requirement
  • Electronic or physical ticket instructions
  • Exchange, refund and cancellation rules
Station names can be similar and some cities have multiple railway stations. Agents should verify the exact station, connection and passenger details before completing a non-refundable or restricted booking.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is RezLive's B2B rail booking platform?

RezLive's B2B rail booking platform helps approved travel professionals arrange supported European train tickets and rail passes for customers through RezLive's partnership with Rail Europe.

How many countries are covered by European Global Rail Passes?

Rail Europe's Eurail and Interrail Global Pass products cover 33 European countries. Point-to-point ticket coverage differs because it depends on the operator, route and timetable.

What is the difference between a train ticket and a rail pass?

A train ticket covers a particular journey or route. A rail pass provides travel rights across a defined country or group of countries for selected travel days and validity.

What is the difference between Eurail and Interrail?

Eurail is intended for travellers who are not European residents. Interrail is intended for citizens or legal residents of European countries.

Does a Eurail or Interrail Pass include seat reservations?

Not always. Some high-speed, international and night trains require a separate reservation even when the traveller holds a valid pass.

Which train operators are represented by Rail Europe?

Rail Europe's agent platform lists more than 200 train providers, including SNCF, Eurostar, SBB, Deutsche Bahn, Trenitalia, Italo, Renfe, ÖBB and National Rail. Exact RezLive availability may vary.

Can European train tickets be exchanged or cancelled?

Exchange and refund conditions depend on the operator, fare, pass product and booking rules. Agents should review the conditions before confirming.

Who can use RezLive's rail booking service?

RezLive is a B2B platform for approved travel agents, OTAs, DMCs, tour operators, wholesalers and other tourism businesses.
