What Is RezLive's B2B Sightseeing Booking Platform?

RezLive Sightseeing is a business-to-business booking platform for tours, attractions and destination activities. It is designed for approved travel professionals who need global sightseeing content for their customers rather than a direct-to-consumer booking website.

RezLive publicly states that its platform provides more than 45,000 sightseeing items and over 5,000 tours across 500 cities. Exact activities, time slots, inclusions and confirmation conditions depend on the destination, travel date and live supplier inventory.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much sightseeing inventory is available through RezLive?

RezLive publicly states that it offers more than 45,000 sightseeing items and over 5,000 tours across 500 cities. Exact products and departure times depend on the selected destination, date and supplier availability.

Does RezLive provide sightseeing activities across the globe?

RezLive provides broad global sightseeing and activity coverage. The page should not claim that every attraction worldwide is available; agents should check live results for the destination and travel date.

Who can use RezLive's sightseeing booking platform?

RezLive is a B2B platform for approved travel agents, OTAs, DMCs, tour operators, wholesalers and other tourism businesses rather than individual travellers.

What types of tours and activities can agents book?

Depending on the destination, available options may include city tours, attraction tickets, cultural experiences, family entertainment, excursions, adventure activities and other local experiences.

What is a sightseeing voucher?

A sightseeing voucher is the booking document issued after the applicable payment and confirmation process. The traveller should present it whenever the activity supplier requests proof of booking.

Can sightseeing bookings be combined with hotels and transfers?

Yes. Travel partners can combine sightseeing with RezLive hotels, transfers, cruises and other available services to create a broader itinerary.

Can a sightseeing booking be cancelled or amended?

Amendment and cancellation rules vary by activity, supplier and rate condition. Agents should check the displayed policy before confirming the booking with their customer.
