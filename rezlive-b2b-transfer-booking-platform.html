What Is RezLive's B2B Transfer Booking Platform?

RezLive Transfers is a business-to-business ground transportation booking service for approved travel professionals. It helps agents arrange customer movement between supported airports, hotels, city locations and intercity destinations instead of leaving travellers to organise transport after arrival.

RezLive publicly states that its platform provides more than 5,000 transfer options across over 900 airport and city locations, including private and shared car transfers. Exact routes, operating hours, vehicle/service categories and confirmation conditions depend on the selected destination, travel date and live supplier inventory.

Why Add Transfers to a Customer Itinerary?

  • Complete the journey: connect the customer's arrival or departure with their hotel, sightseeing or onward destination.
  • Plan before travel: reduce the need for customers to arrange unfamiliar local transport after they arrive.
  • Private and shared options: compare the service types available for the selected route and budget.
  • B2B booking workflow: manage the service as part of the travel agent's broader RezLive itinerary.
  • Clear booking documentation: provide the applicable transfer voucher, pickup information and supplier instructions.
  • Cross-sell travel services: combine transfers with hotels, sightseeing, cruise and other available RezLive products.

Information to Check Before Booking a Transfer

Accurate travel and contact information is particularly important for airport pickups and time-sensitive departure transfers.

  • Exact pickup point and full address
  • Exact drop-off point and destination
  • Transfer date and required time
  • Flight number and arrival or departure schedule
  • Passenger count and child ages
  • Number and size of luggage pieces
  • Lead traveller's working mobile number
  • Private or shared service preference
  • Meeting-point and driver-contact instructions
  • Amendment, cancellation and no-show conditions
For airport arrivals, agents should enter the correct flight details. For hotel-to-airport departures, follow the confirmed supplier's displayed pickup time and instructions rather than assuming the traveller's preferred departure time.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a B2B transfer booking platform?

A B2B transfer booking platform allows approved travel businesses to search and book pre-arranged ground transportation for their customers between supported pickup and drop-off points.

How much transfer inventory is available through RezLive?

RezLive publicly states that it provides more than 5,000 transfer options across over 900 airport and city locations. Exact routes and services depend on live supplier availability.

Can agents book airport-to-hotel and hotel-to-airport transfers?

Agents can search supported airport and city transfer routes, including airport-to-hotel and hotel-to-airport requirements, subject to availability for the selected location and date.

Does RezLive offer private and shared transfers?

Yes. RezLive publicly describes its inventory as including private and shared car transfers. The service formats shown depend on the selected route and supplier.

Can RezLive be used for intercity or outstation transfers?

Travel agents can search supported city and intercity transfer requirements. Availability should be confirmed for the exact pickup point, destination, travel date and passenger count.

What information is required for an airport transfer?

Agents should provide accurate pickup and drop-off details, travel date, passenger and luggage information, a working customer contact number and the correct flight number and schedule.

What is a transfer voucher?

A transfer voucher is the booking document issued after the applicable payment and confirmation process. The traveller should keep it available and follow the pickup instructions shown on it.

Can a transfer booking be amended or cancelled?

Amendment and cancellation rules vary by route, supplier and booking condition. Agents should check the displayed policy before confirming the service.
