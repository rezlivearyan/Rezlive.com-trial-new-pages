About RezLive: A Global B2B Travel Booking Platform

RezLive.com is a globally acclaimed B2B online reservation system designed for professional travel partners who need reliable access to global travel inventory.

RezLive is a B2B travel booking platform by Travel Designer Group that helps travel agents, OTAs, DMCs, tour operators, wholesalers and corporate travel businesses search, compare and book hotels, sightseeing, transfers, rail, cruises, car rentals and other travel services for their customers.

Built for Travel Agents, OTAs, DMCs and Tour Operators

Launched in 2007 in India, RezLive.com is the flagship product of Travel Designer Group. From its beginnings in Ahmedabad, the business has developed into a global B2B travel platform serving professional tourism partners across the Middle East, South Asia, Southeast Asia and other international markets.

Travel agents and travel companies use RezLive to arrange leisure, corporate, group and customised travel requirements. The platform is designed for trade users rather than direct consumers, helping approved travel partners access relevant inventory, compare booking conditions and manage services for their clients through the RezLive Point of Sales website, mobile solutions and available connectivity options.

RezLive's Global Travel Inventory

RezLive connects travel partners with an extensive selection of accommodation, destination experiences and mobility products. Award-Winning B2B Travel Technology by Travel Designer Group

RezLive.com has been recognised as Asia's and India's Leading B2B Travel Provider at the World Travel Tech Awards from 2021 to 2024, and as World's Leading B2B Travel Provider at the World Travel Awards 2018. These recognitions reflect the platform's focus on travel-trade technology, global reach, partner support and continuous product development.

Whether the requirement is leisure, business or group travel, RezLive helps travel professionals find and arrange suitable travel products for their clients through one B2B reservation ecosystem.

Global Reach and Travel-Trade Relationships

RezLive reports a network of more than 40,000 travel partners across over 60 countries. Its international presence, local market teams and post-booking support structure help the company work with travel businesses that have different source markets, customer profiles and operational requirements.

The platform's B2B focus enables RezLive to support independent agents, established agencies, destination management companies, online travel businesses and technology-led travel sellers.
