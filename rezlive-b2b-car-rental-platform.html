What Is RezLive's B2B Car Rental Booking Platform?

RezLive Car Rental is a business-to-business self-drive vehicle booking service for approved travel professionals. It allows agents to search available cars for a selected pickup location, return location, date and time, then review the rate rules before booking for a customer.

RezLive publicly states that its car rental product covers more than 30,000 locations from over 500 rental companies. Exact companies, vehicles, prices and confirmation conditions depend on the requested market, pickup location, rental dates and live supplier inventory.

Vehicle Categories from Economy to Premium

Rental offers are normally organised by vehicle category rather than by a guaranteed make and model. Depending on supplier inventory, available categories may include:

Mini Economy Compact Intermediate Standard Full Size Premium Luxury Small SUV Medium SUV Large SUV Minivan Passenger Van Estate / Wagon Convertible Electric or Hybrid
Use wording such as “Toyota Corolla or similar,” not “guaranteed Toyota Corolla,” unless the supplier explicitly guarantees the model. Agents should compare passenger capacity, baggage space, transmission, fuel or power type and air-conditioning information.

Car Rental vs Transfer: What Is the Difference?

Feature Car Rental Transfer
Who drives? The eligible customer or registered additional driver normally drives the rental vehicle. A transfer generally includes a professional driver supplied for the booked journey.
Duration The vehicle is booked for a rental period and can be used according to the agreement. The service is booked for a specific pickup and drop-off journey.
Best suited to Independent travel, road trips, business mobility and flexible multi-stop itineraries. Airport pickups, hotel drops and customers who do not want to drive.
Main checks Licence, age, deposit, insurance, fuel, mileage, extras and rental-location rules. Pickup time, flight details, passenger count, luggage and meeting instructions.

What Agents Should Check Before Confirming a Rental Car

  • Lead driver's full name and correct age
  • Valid driving licence requirements
  • International Driving Permit requirements
  • Passport or identification requirements
  • Accepted card and security-deposit amount
  • Pickup location and opening hours
  • Vehicle category, passengers and baggage
  • Manual or automatic transmission
  • Included and excluded protection
  • Fuel policy and refuelling charges
  • Unlimited or limited mileage
  • One-way rental and return-location fees
  • Cross-border or ferry restrictions
  • Additional driver and child-seat charges
  • Late collection and after-hours fees
  • Cancellation, amendment and no-show rules
Driver-age rules are supplier- and country-specific. Young-driver or senior-driver charges may apply, so the correct driver's age should be included in the search before the rate is shown to the customer.

Understanding Car Rental Rates and Conditions

The lowest displayed rate is not the only factor a travel agent should compare. The final recommendation should reflect the complete rental conditions.

₹

Rate Inclusions

Check taxes, local fees, mileage, protection and any services included in the selected offer.

D

Security Deposit

Confirm the deposit amount, accepted card type and whether the card must be in the lead driver's name.

F

Fuel Policy

Review how the vehicle must be returned and what charges apply when the required fuel level is not met.

KM

Mileage Allowance

Check whether mileage is unlimited or capped per day or rental, and the cost of additional distance.

+

Optional Extras

Additional drivers, child seats, navigation and other equipment may be requestable and may carry extra fees.

X

Cancellation Rules

Cancellation and no-show terms vary by offer and supplier; display the exact rule attached to the selected rate.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is RezLive's B2B car rental platform?

RezLive's B2B car rental platform helps approved travel professionals search and book self-drive rental cars for customers across supported airport, city and rental locations.

How much car rental coverage does RezLive offer?

RezLive publicly states that its car rental product covers more than 30,000 locations from over 500 rental companies. Exact availability depends on the requested market, date and supplier inventory.

What is the difference between car rental and transfer?

A rental car is normally driven by the eligible customer for a booked period. A transfer is a pre-arranged pickup-to-drop-off service and generally includes a driver.

Can agents book airport and downtown car rentals?

Agents can search supported airport and city rental locations. The exact office, opening hours and collection conditions should be checked before confirmation.

Can customers return the car to a different city or location?

A one-way rental may be offered on selected routes. The supplier may charge an additional one-way fee, which should be reviewed before booking.

Is the displayed car model guaranteed?

Usually the booking is for a vehicle category. The displayed model is often an example, and the supplier may provide that model or a similar car in the same category.

What documents are needed to collect a rental car?

Requirements vary, but commonly include a valid driving licence, passport or identification, the booking voucher and a card accepted for the security deposit.

Why must the agent enter the driver's age?

Suppliers may have minimum and maximum age requirements or apply young-driver and senior-driver charges. The correct age helps return the applicable rate.

What conditions should agents check before booking?

Check the inclusions, protection, deposit, mileage, fuel policy, licence and age rules, extras, opening hours, cross-border conditions, one-way fees and cancellation policy.
