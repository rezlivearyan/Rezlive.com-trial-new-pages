RezLive combines online cruise booking for selected cruise lines with a supported enquiry process for additional brands. Travel partners can use the platform to explore suitable sailings, gather the required passenger and cabin information, and connect cruise reservations with hotels, transfers, sightseeing and other travel services.

What Is RezLive's B2B Cruise Booking Service?

RezLive Cruise is a business-to-business cruise booking service for travel professionals. Selected cruise inventory can be searched and booked online, while other cruise lines can be handled through an assisted or offline quotation workflow, subject to sailing, cabin, market and supplier availability.

The hybrid model helps travel agents manage straightforward online bookings while still receiving human support for cruise products that require detailed itinerary checks, cabin selection, passenger-age validation or manual confirmation.

Why Travel Agents Use RezLive for Cruise Enquiries

  • Hybrid booking workflow: online search for selected cruise lines plus assisted support for additional brands.
  • B2B-focused process: designed for approved travel agents, OTAs, DMCs, tour operators and wholesalers.
  • Complete trip planning: connect a cruise with pre- or post-cruise hotels, transfers, sightseeing, rail and car rental.
  • Support for complex enquiries: obtain help with cabin categories, passenger ages, itinerary details, inclusions and booking conditions.
  • Clearer client servicing: collect the right information before quotation and reduce avoidable back-and-forth with travellers.

Information Needed for a Cruise Quotation

Agents can improve response time and quotation accuracy by sharing the following details in the first enquiry:

  • Preferred cruise line or open-to-suggestions request
  • Departure port, destination or preferred region
  • Travel dates and acceptable date flexibility
  • Number of nights or preferred cruise duration
  • Number of adults, children and passenger ages
  • Cabin preference: interior, ocean-view, balcony or suite
  • Approximate budget and preferred currency
  • Pre- or post-cruise hotel and transfer requirements

Frequently Asked Questions

Which cruise lines can travel agents book through RezLive?

Selected Norwegian Cruise Line and StarDream Cruises inventory is intended for online booking. Assisted enquiries may be submitted for Disney Cruise Line, Royal Caribbean, Cordelia Cruises and other requirements, subject to internal commercial access and sailing availability.

What is the difference between online and assisted cruise booking?

Online booking lets an agent search and process enabled cruise inventory through the platform. Assisted booking requires the agent to submit trip details so the RezLive team can check the requested cruise product and provide the next steps.

Is RezLive Cruise available to individual travellers?

RezLive is a B2B platform for approved travel professionals. Individual travellers should book through a registered travel agency or other authorised seller.

Can RezLive arrange hotels and transfers around a cruise?

Travel agents can use RezLive's hotel, transfer, sightseeing and other service pages to build pre-cruise and post-cruise arrangements, subject to availability.

What details should an agent provide for an assisted cruise enquiry?

Provide the preferred cruise line or region, departure port, travel dates, duration, passenger count and ages, cabin preference, budget and any hotel or transfer requirements.

Are cruise fares and cancellation rules the same for every sailing?

No. Fares, deposits, payment deadlines, inclusions, amendment charges and cancellation conditions can vary by cruise line, sailing, cabin category, promotion and market. Agents should verify the final conditions before confirming with the traveller.
